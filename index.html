The process

Three steps to the right support

No endless directories. No confusing insurance calls. Just a clear, personal path to someone who can actually help.

01

Tell us what you need

Fill out our short intake form — no clinical jargon, no pressure, no commitment. Just honest questions about what you're going through and what matters to you.

02

We do the matching

Our team personally reviews your needs and finds providers that truly fit — your goals, your budget, your schedule, and your insurance situation. Real humans, real care.

03

You make the call

We send a warm introduction to the right provider. The hardest part is deciding to get help — not finding who to call. We make sure you get there.

Why we exist

The system makes it too hard to ask for help

The barrier isn't willingness — it's friction. We exist to remove every obstacle between someone and the support they need.

Outdated directories

Insurance provider lists are often months out of date — full of therapists who aren't accepting patients or don't take your plan.

Phone tag and wait times

Even when you find someone, you're leaving voicemails, waiting weeks, and starting over when they don't call back.

Insurance confusion

Deductibles, copays, in-network vs out-of-network — navigating insurance while already struggling is exhausting and often stops people cold.

No personal fit

Finding a provider isn't just about availability — it's about fit. Specialty, approach, personality, and cultural background all matter deeply.

The hardest part wasn't wanting help. It was not knowing where to start — and giving up before ever finding it.
