Our difference

Why Headstrong Haven is different

Every decision we make starts with one question: does this make it easier for someone to get help? Here's what that looks like in practice.

Real humans do the matching

No algorithm decides who you talk to. Our team personally reviews every intake and matches you based on your actual situation — not just keywords and availability slots.

Human-first

Your data stays yours — always

BetterHelp was fined by the FTC for sharing users' sensitive mental health data with advertisers. We will never sell, share, or monetize your personal information. Ever.

Privacy guaranteed

Free for everyone seeking care

BetterHelp charges $280–$400 per month. We charge clients nothing. Mental health support shouldn't have a paywall. Our providers fund the platform so you never have to.

Always free for clients

Providers are real and verified

Psychology Today lets anyone pay to list. We personally review every provider application, verify credentials, and only list providers we would refer our own family to.

Verified network

Local and community-rooted

The big platforms are national and impersonal. We're starting in Cleveland and growing community by community — because local trust, local providers, and local knowledge matter.

Community-first

Crisis support built in from day one

Every page on our platform surfaces the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. We built safety into the foundation — not as an afterthought, but as a core value.

Safety-first
