Before we begin, you matter

If you or someone you know is in crisis or needs immediate support, please reach out now. Help is always available — free, confidential, and 24/7.

🆘 Need help right now?

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of suicide or self-harm, or feel unsafe — please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline immediately. You are not alone and you deserve support right now.

Call or Text 988 — Crisis Lifeline
