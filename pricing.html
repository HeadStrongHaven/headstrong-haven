Frequently asked questions

Billing has not started yet — we are currently in our free founding phase. We will give all providers at least 30 days notice before any billing begins, and founding members will be locked into the Starter rate of $99/month.

Yes — always. Headstrong Haven will never charge people seeking mental health support. Our mission is to remove barriers to care, and cost is one of the biggest barriers. Providers fund the platform so clients never have to.

A referral is when we send you a warm introduction to a client who has been matched to your profile. It includes their intake information, what they are looking for, and a personal note from our team. What happens next is between you and the client.

Yes. There are no contracts or long-term commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time and you will not be charged for the following month. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you in.

When a client fills out our intake form, our team personally reviews their needs and matches them against our provider network — considering specialty, location, availability, session format, insurance, and personal fit. It is a human-driven process, not an algorithm. We only send you a referral when we are confident it is a genuine match.

When billing launches, we will accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe, which is one of the most secure payment platforms available. We do not store any card information ourselves. We will also explore ACH bank transfer options for Enterprise plans.
